Our Service

During these tough times, medical care has become a top most priority. To respond to this growing need, our talented team of telecommunication experts have set up a streamlined communications platform for you to keep in touch with a doctor. Our service eliminates middle-men, prioritizes communication between you and your doctor, and proctects your privacy all in one.

24 Hour Support

You can dial our Emergency Assistance Line 24 hours a day, 7 days a week if you have a medical problem and cannot reach your physician immediately, or you are not sure if you can call us otherwise. This advantage lets you talk with an experienced licensed health care provider after you've enrolled to get answers to your questions and see what actions you should take. You can notice the 24-Hour Emergency Assistance Lines phone number at the back of the Participant ID card if you are not a member of the Independent Well-being Card.

Emergency Services

For our members, emergency services in are given free of charge. For patients who are under financial strain and unable to pay for our services, Local ambulances also have a program in place. 24 hours required for the patient transfer vehicle. Patients are taken to hospitals from home (on campus) and patients are referred to the hospital by emergency duty doctors.

Medical Counseling

The process of providing information, advice and assistance to patients to improve their health, adherence to treatment and quality of life; The emphasis is on open dialogue between the patient and the doctor, collaborative decision-making and a common purpose of pain management for the patient. Takes into account patients' individual wishes, concerns, and emotions. Our experts are dedicated to giving you their undivided attention to provide you with real and effective communication over your health.



Privacy

Privacy is one of our core values. We believe that every person has the right to protect their data, and we have taken multiple measure to uphold this. Our email services are backed by Proton Mail's state-of-the-art Swiss privacy standards to ensure nobody--not even the government--can peek in on your conversations. All other data is held in a local server which utilizes modern privacy technologies such as salting to make sure that your data is immune to cyber-attacks.
